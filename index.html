    vendy is hyperlocal community

    At the heart of Vendy is the idea that community is what makes us human. We want to connect you with local vendors and small bussiness owners in your community! Join us and see who's selling around you!

    How can you join?

    Vendy has two different ways to join right now. As a regular member, or as a vendor. Regular members have access to a buyers account. There they can enter their information and be connected to relavent vendors nearby. Vendor's have access to everything a a regular account does, but also have the abillity to open and maintain a online marketplace.

  • Local Member

    You are the very reason why people come to this app and open shop. local members make things happen, they are the reason our vendors wake up every morning and run the shops they do. Vendy was built for people like you.

  • Local Vendor

    We made this app for you. Trying to get your brand out can be really hard and often times a waste of money. Why spend money on marketing and advertisement, when you can invest in actualy meaningful interactions with people around you. Vendy connects you with people that want to shop your inventory.
